# ADR-0005: AI Integration Roadmap

## Status

Accepted

## Context

BailMate is designed as a real-time coordination platform connecting individuals seeking bail assistance with licensed bail bond providers. As the platform grows, artificial intelligence can improve speed, decision support, and operational efficiency while keeping providers in control of final decisions.

## Decision

Adopt a phased approach to AI integration, introducing AI capabilities only after the core dispatch and lead management workflows are stable and well understood.

## Rationale

AI should augment provider decision-making rather than automate critical business decisions.

Planned AI capabilities include:

- Intelligent provider recommendations
- Lead prioritization
- Duplicate request detection
- Fraud signal detection
- Conversation summarization
- Follow-up message drafting
- Predictive demand analytics
- Provider performance insights

## Guiding Principles

- Human-in-the-loop decision making
- Transparent recommendations
- Explainable AI outputs
- Privacy-first architecture
- Secure handling of customer information
- Continuous evaluation and improvement

## Tradeoffs

Introducing AI increases system complexity and requires careful monitoring to ensure recommendations remain reliable, fair, and understandable.

## Future Improvements

Long-term, BailMate may evolve into an AI-assisted operations platform that helps providers:

- Manage leads
- Optimize daily workflows
- Forecast demand
- Generate operational reports
- Surface actionable business insights

AI will remain an advisory system, with licensed providers responsible for operational decisions.
